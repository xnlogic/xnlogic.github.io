Why did we build the xnlogic platform?

Darrick, the co-founder of xnlogic has been developing business solutions for 15 years, and in that time he has seen the need for the same pieces of foundational software over and over again. We'll let him explain his motivations in his own words.

Each time I've built those same pieces, my frustration increased that instead of being able to tackle the most valuable and interesting parts of the project, I had to solve the same basic problems. And despite using a variety of the most popular and innovative tools, they really did only a very small part of the job - the easiest part - before leaving me on my own to solve the most challenging aspects of these same problems on my own. Despite being extremely commonplace, there are no simple solutions to these problems.
